Wireless Keyboard Firmware

ZOROKB Firmware and Documentation Hub

ZOROKB is a keyboard firmware written in C for mechanical and electrocapacitive boards, powered by nRF52833 and the more capable nRF54LM20A. It delivers low-power, low-latency, Vial-configurable keyboard experiences.

Multi-protocol USB, BLE, and 2.4G managed in one firmware stack
Native Vial Real-time remapping, layers, macros, and advanced actions
Production-ready Suitable for daily use, after-sales support, and firmware delivery

Core capabilities

Built around reliable connectivity, flexible configuration, and easier product delivery.

MX / EC support

Supports both mechanical and electrocapacitive product lines with hardware-aware firmware behavior.

Native Vial

Edit keys, layers, macros, and advanced actions in real time without reflashing.

BLE multi-device

Switch among three Bluetooth devices for desktop, tablet, and mobile workflows.

2.4G channel routing

Keep receiver channels and keyboard channels in sync for office and gaming scenarios.

Long battery life

With a 1000mAh battery and lighting off, mechanical boards can reach around 1000h, EC boards about 400h.

Simple updates

Flash new UF2 builds through drag and drop to reduce maintenance overhead.
